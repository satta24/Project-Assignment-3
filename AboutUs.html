About Us

This website is mainly intended to educate people about the Covid-19 pandemic through providing overall information about the virus and its variants. Moreover, this website provides some up to date statistics and data about the daily cases and deaths caused by the virus. In addition, the website provides information on ways to stay safe and protected from the virus. Finally, the website offers some extra resources for individuals intressted to learn more about Covid-19, the vaccinees avaliable and more.
